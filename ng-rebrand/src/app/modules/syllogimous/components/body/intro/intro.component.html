Welcome to Syllogimous v4

Syllogimous v4 is an advanced cognitive game that takes logical reasoning to the next level. Building on traditional syllogisms, this game challenges you with a variety of logical tasks including analogies, temporal and spatial reasoning, and binary questions.

The game features an adaptive level system that adjusts the difficulty based on your performance. With increasing complexity, a variety of relational frames, and multiple tier levels, Syllogimous v4 ensures that you are constantly challenged and improving your cognitive skills.

Why You Should Play

Studies have provided compelling evidence linking cognitive enhancement to relational training based on Relational Frame Theory (RFT). RFT is a psychological theory of human language and cognition that posits the ability to mentally relate things in abstract ways as foundational to higher cognitive processes such as language, problem-solving, and learning.

These findings offer a promising avenue for educational and psychological interventions aimed at boosting intellectual functioning. The implications are profound, suggesting that cognitive abilities can be significantly and deliberately enhanced through well-structured relational training programs, potentially leading to better educational outcomes and more effective problem-solving skills in everyday life.
